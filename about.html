About the Syracuse Bakers Expo

Discover the story behind the celebration of Central New York's baking community.

The Bakers Expo, organized by Syracuse city councilor Rita Paniagua and Trita Treatz owner Sherita Semple, aims to promote small businesses and individual bakers who want to grow their businesses.

The idea for the event came following a large federal grant given to CNY Arts for the purpose of rebuilding the Syracuse community in the wake of the pandemic.

Many bakers lost their shops because of COVID-19, so Paniagua and Semple came up with the idea for the expo to help the community get back on its feet.

Now, years later, the project continues to grow to include an even larger audience.

We can't wait to gather the community and celebrate together.
